What the App Can Do for You

Sends and receives App-to-App messages, emails, and SMS messages from any location to anyone, anywhere on earth.

Seamlessly and automatically communicates back and forth whether you’re off the grid or inside Wi-Fi or cellular coverage.

Provides detailed weather information to help you stay safe and make better decisions.

Allows you to communicate critical details with search and rescue officials in the event of an emergency.

What the App Can Do for Your Friends, Family and Colleagues
Provides them with a convenient, hassle-free method of always reaching you regardless of your location or cellular coverage. Send them an invitation to join you from inside the settings tab of the App or the “Devices and Plans” tab of the online account portal.
Allows them to send you lengthier App-to-App messages (up to 950 characters), as opposed to only 160 characters through SMS using your designated ZOLEO number and 270 characters using your ZOLEO email address.
In the event of an emergency, ZOLEO gives them peace of mind knowing you will have direct communication with GEOS,our search and rescue monitoring partner. Should you trigger an SOS, GEOS will contact your SOS contacts and keep them updated with rescue details.
What Happens Without the App
If your smartphone becomes damaged or runs out of battery life, your ZOLEO device will still continue to send and receive data, but you will not be able to see your messages or send a custom reply.

Here’s what you can do without the App:

Send a Check-In message (with optional location information) to let others know you’re okay.

Trigger an SOS alert ( in the event of an emergency and summon search and rescue resources.

Once you are able to re-connect your device to your smartphone, the ZOLEO App will display any queued incoming messages.
